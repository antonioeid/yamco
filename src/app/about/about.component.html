Propane vs Butane whatshot

You think you have worked out what you want, then you discover there are two types of LPG (Liqueified Petroleum Gas) – Butane and Propane.

Propane:

Propane is widely used over butane when camping or climbing in cold temperatures, because of its lower boiling point that keeps it a gas even in very low temperatures. Therefore, it is a better material for exterior usage. Some of the common uses of propane are heating systems, either domestic or commercial ones. It is also known to be used in restaurants, resorts and hospitals because it exerts much higher pressure compared to butane gas.

Butane:

Butane is more appropriate for interior usage because it exerts less pressure. It is cheaper than propane gas, and it is mostly used in houses for cooking, baking and portable heaters.
